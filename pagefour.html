Personal Profile

I enjoyed reading the results of these tests. I'm very curious how answering some questions can create such an in depth analysis of my personality!

As I completed each of these personality tests, I noticed I seemed to be getting results that seemed pretty consistent with each other. My results showed that I was someone who looked out for other people and ensured that they were happy and comfortable, often putting their needs ahead of my own

These results, more or less, showed me what I already new. As the oldest child, I took it as my responsibity to make sure my younger siblings were happy and safe. I believe that this has been something I've carried throughout my life to the people around me.
